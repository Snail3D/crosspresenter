The Team

Two guys, one mission

A worship leader who spots what's missing and an agentic coder who ships what he envisions.

EW
Eric Woodard
Co-Founder, Engineering

Eric Woodard is an evangelist, father of two daughters, entrepreneur, and agentic coder since 2024. He has a passion for systems architecture, streamlining, and automation, not for the sake of technology itself, but for what it gives back to people.

His goal isn't to replace the crew. It's to free them. Too many Sunday mornings, the tech team is stuck behind screens, heads down, managing slides and cues while the worship happens around them. Eric believes it shouldn't be that way. The people running the technology should be able to worship alongside the congregation, present in the moment, while the systems handle the timing. Let the crew give direction and still be in the room. Let them serve and worship at the same time.

That's the heart behind Luther's Code: technology that steps back so people can step forward.

JW
Judah Webb
Co-Founder, Product & Ministry

Judah Webb grew up in ministry. With parents deeply involved in ministry work for decades, church life isn't something Judah stumbled into. It's the air he's breathed since childhood. By the time most kids were figuring out what they wanted to do, Judah was already serving in church ministry, learning the rhythms of worship, tech, and production from the ground up.

Today, Judah serves as worship leader, bringing over two decades of hands-on ministry experience to everything he touches. He's the rare blend of creative and technical, equally at home leading a congregation in worship as he is behind a soundboard, designing stage sets, or building production workflows that make Sunday mornings run seamlessly.

His superpower is spotting what's missing. Where others see a smooth service, Judah sees the friction. The manual slide clicks, the fumbled transitions, the moments where technology gets in the way of worship instead of enabling it. That instinct for "this could be better" is what drives Luther's Code.

Judah lives with his wife and their young daughter.
